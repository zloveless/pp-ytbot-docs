# Welcome to ytbot

**ytbot** is a custom YouTube chat integration bot for the [@pseudoposse](https://youtube.com/@pseudoposse) *7 Days to Die* streams. It lets viewers interact with the game in real time — similar to Twitch Integration — by spending accumulated points to trigger in-game events.

You can **help** the streamers with heals and buffs, **hinder** them with debuffs and injuries, or **unleash chaos** by spawning zombies and hostile creatures directly on top of them.
